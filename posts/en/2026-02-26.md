# From Japan: Enterprise-Exclusive AI Buddy "RiN Family" Officially Launches

Metelix Corporation officially launched "RiN Family," a service that cultivates and provides exclusive AI buddies tailored to individual enterprises, on February 22nd. This service is characterized by the fact that a customized AI agent called "RiN" grows from scratch to match each company's operations, culture, and team.

Unlike general-purpose AI tools, the greatest strength of this service is that it takes root in the adopting company and **works as a partner to executives and employees**. While previous AI tools required "the user to adapt," RiN Family achieves the reverse concept of "the AI adapts to the enterprise."

[→ Read the original article (ASCII.jp)](https://ascii.jp/elem/000/004/376/4376582/?rss)

*Impact on our lives*: In the future, it may become commonplace for not just large corporations but also small and medium-sized enterprises to have AI partners tailored to their corporate culture.

## Management-Level AI Dialogue Training "AI Management Dojo" Debuts

U-M Technology Japan Inc. announced the launch of "AI Management Dojo," an AI-powered dialogue training partner for managers. This service visualizes managers' dialogue skills through exercises with AI and aims to improve the quality of dialogue in one-on-one meetings with subordinates.

By supporting **standardization of management quality across the entire organization**, what was traditionally dependent on individual skills—"good manager management"—can now be learned by anyone through AI. Practical training utilizing LLMs (large language models) may resolve skill bottlenecks among managers.

*Impact on our lives*: If workplace relationships are improved through AI coaching, work-related stress could be significantly reduced.

## Google Integrates Robotics Software Company Intrinsic Under Its Wing

Intrinsic, an Alphabet-owned robotics software company, has been announced to be integrated under Google. Intrinsic had spun off from Alphabet approximately five years ago, but through this integration into Google, **further fusion of AI and robotics is expected to accelerate**.

This move signifies the combination of generative AI technology and robotic control, accelerating the development of increasingly automated industrial robots. As demand for automation in manufacturing increases, such integration is likely to have a major impact on the entire industry.

[→ Read the original article (TechCrunch AI)](https://techcrunch.com/2026/02/25/alphabet-owned-robotics-software-company-intrinsic-joins-google/)

*Impact on our lives*: As robots become more intelligent, automation of dangerous and repetitive tasks will advance, allowing humans to focus on more creative work.

## Nvidia Reports Record Quarterly Results, Token Demand "Completely Exponential"

Nvidia announced record quarterly results. Jensen Huang, the company's CEO, commented that "global token demand is growing completely exponentially." Tokens refer to the basic units of characters and words that LLMs (large language models) process.

This statement indicates that the usage of generative AI is expanding at an unstoppable pace, revealing that **demand for AI inference (the process by which a model makes predictions from new data) is exploding**. Accompanying this, Nvidia's sales of GPUs (graphics processing units) for data centers continue to perform well.

[→ Read the original article (TechCrunch AI)](https://techcrunch.com/2026/02/25/nvidia-earnings-record-capex-spend-ai/)

*Impact on our lives*: As investment by AI companies continues to expand, more advanced AI features will be integrated into apps and services.

## Samsung Galaxy S26 Also Enhanced: "Now AI" Suggests Next Operations

Samsung announced the new "Galaxy S26" smartphone series, scheduled to launch domestically on March 12th. As a new feature, it features **"Now AI," which suggests the next operation based on the situation**.

Additionally, a new *privacy display* technology that prevents screen peeping has been implemented, with further integration with AI (Gemini). This demonstrates that the era of native-level AI integration on smartphones is beginning in earnest.

## White House Demands AI Companies Bear Burden of Rising Electricity Costs

The White House made an announcement requesting that AI companies bear the burden of rising electricity costs. During the State of the Union address, President Trump revealed that he is in negotiations with major tech companies over a "rate payer protection pledge," and stated that multiple companies have already made public commitments regarding electricity cost burden-sharing.

This can be seen as a measure addressing **social concerns about the increased electricity consumption of AI companies**. As data center power demand continues to increase, discussions on corporate social responsibility are now taking place at the political level.

[→ Read the original article (TechCrunch AI)](https://techcrunch.com/2026/02/25/the-white-house-wants-ai-companies-to-cover-rate-hikes-most-have-already-said-they-would/)

*Impact on our lives*: If electricity cost burden-sharing by AI companies becomes mandatory, it may help mitigate rising electricity rates for ordinary households.

## Summary

From the AI-related news of February 25th emerges a major trend: **"the transition to an AI implementation phase."**

Domestically in Japan, practical AI solutions addressing specific business challenges are appearing one after another, such as the enterprise-exclusive AI agent "RiN Family" and management-level training "AI Management Dojo." Meanwhile, globally, Google integrates robotics under its umbrella and Nvidia achieves record growth, with investment in AI infrastructure accelerating.

From AI integration on smartphones (Galaxy S26's "Now AI") to enterprise automation tools (MCP adoption), AI is steadily permeating society. At the same time, electricity consumption issues are becoming apparent, with questions being raised about the social responsibility of AI companies.

2026 will be a pivotal period where "democratization of AI adoption" and "response to social challenges" advance simultaneously.
