## OpenAI Robotics Division Leader Resigns Over Pentagon Contract Objections

Caitlin Kalinowski, who led OpenAI's robotics division, resigned from her position in protest of the company's contract with the U.S. Department of Defense. This decision suggests growing concerns within the industry regarding the military applications of AI technology.

OpenAI is a rapidly growing company, but in the process faces various social challenges. The ethical debate surrounding the use of advanced AI technologies like robotics for defense purposes will become an important theme in future industrial development.

**Impact on our lives:** The ongoing debate about ethical applications of AI technology may significantly influence policy decisions across companies going forward.

## ChatGPT's "Adult Mode" Delayed Again

OpenAI announced that it would further delay the rollout of ChatGPT's "adult mode" feature, which would allow adult users to access adult-oriented content. The feature was originally scheduled for release in December, marking the second delay.

Defining appropriate usage boundaries for AI is an important issue not only for companies but for society as a whole. Large language models (LLMs) like ChatGPT must accommodate various use cases, and content policies are repeatedly reviewed in this process.

[→ Read the original article (TechCrunch)](https://techcrunch.com/2026/03/07/openai-delays-chatgpts-adult-mode-again/)

**Impact on our lives:** The scope and timing of AI service offerings are determined through a balance between corporate decisions and societal needs.

## Grammarly's "Expert Review" Feature Faces Criticism

The "Expert Review" feature recently added by Grammarly, an AI grammar correction tool, has faced criticism. The feature claims to provide guidance from renowned writers and thinkers worldwide, but it has been pointed out that expert involvement is insufficient.

Grammarly aims to improve users' writing quality using natural language processing (NLP) technology. However, when claiming to offer "expert insights," the gap between the underlying technology and reality must be transparently explained to consumers.

[→ Read the original article (TechCrunch)](https://techcrunch.com/2026/03/07/grammarlys-expert-review-is-just-missing-the-actual-experts/)

**Impact on our lives:** The reliability and transparency of AI features will become increasingly important as user decision-making criteria going forward.

## Sundar Pichai Receives $692 Million CEO Compensation Package

Google has awarded Sundar Pichai, its Chief Executive Officer, a compensation package worth $692 million. The majority of it is performance-based and includes new stock-based incentives tied to the performance of autonomous vehicle company Waymo and small delivery drone company Wing.

This executive appointment demonstrates that Google is pursuing strategic growth in multiple AI-related domains—generative AI, autonomous driving, and robotics. Since top-level executive compensation reflects corporate priorities, attention is focused on the future direction of Google's subsidiary companies.

[→ Read the original article (TechCrunch)](https://techcrunch.com/2026/03/07/google-just-gave-sundar-pichai-a-692m-pay-package/)

**Impact on our lives:** Through Google's major investments, AI-applied technologies such as autonomous driving and drone delivery may accelerate rapidly, potentially making our lives increasingly convenient.

Today's AI industry is moving forward with the major theme of balancing ethical issues and technological advancement.
