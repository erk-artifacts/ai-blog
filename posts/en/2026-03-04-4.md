## Raycast Announces "Glaze," Enabling Code-Free App Development with Generative AI

U.S. startup Raycast has announced "Glaze," an innovative coding platform that leverages **generative AI (such as Claude Code)**. The platform's most distinctive feature is that **software development is possible using only natural language, without requiring programming knowledge**.

Glaze Store functions as a directory where users can share and explore "vibe code" created by other users. While LLM (large language model) tools like Claude Code have made it easy for anyone to develop applications, the implementation process has remained complex. However, Glaze aims to greatly simplify this process and realize a **truly democratized app development environment**.

**Impact on Our Lives:** As environments that enable app and tool creation without programming experience become available, an era is approaching where anyone can immediately digitalize their ideas.

## Infrastructure Innovation in the AI Era: Offshore Floating Data Centers Solve Cooling Challenges

The advent of the AI era has made data center power demand and cooling problems the industry's greatest challenges. Aikido, an offshore wind power operator, plans to implement an **innovative solution of positioning AI data centers directly beneath floating offshore wind turbines by 2026**.

This approach, combined with renewable energy, achieves **significant reductions in CO2 emissions and dramatic improvements in energy efficiency**. Additionally, because seawater cooling can be utilized for data center cooling, it has the potential to fundamentally solve the challenges of conventional land-based data centers. The shift away from space-based data center concepts toward a **more practical and environmentally conscious infrastructure strategy** suggests industry maturation.

**Impact on Our Lives:** As the enormous power required for AI processing is supplied by renewable energy, the realization of a more sustainable AI society draws closer.
