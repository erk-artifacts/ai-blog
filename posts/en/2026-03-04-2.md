### 1. "Advanced Techniques" to Unlock AI's Potential

First, here are techniques where "just a little creativity dramatically improves accuracy."

* **Have AI create prompts (meta-prompting) ※Introduced in a previous article.**
    * **Method:** "I want to create a plan document about XX. To help you produce the best possible output, please create a format for the prompt (instructions) that you want me to input."
    * **Effect:** AI extracts necessary elements you might not think of (target audience, budget, timeline, etc.), resulting in high-precision output.
* **Have AI ask counter-questions**
    * **Method:** Add at the end of your instructions: "**If there is missing information or unclear points in executing this instruction, please ask me questions.**"
    * **Effect:** AI points out omissions in your instructions, preventing off-target responses.
* **Make it think step-by-step (Chain of Thought)**
    * **Method:** When asking about complex problems or calculations, add: "**Please think through this logically step by step.**"
    * **Effect:** AI breaks down its thinking process for processing, dramatically improving the accuracy and persuasiveness of conclusions.
* **Provide examples (Few-shot Prompting)**
    * **Method:** "Output in the following manner: [Example 1] [Example 2]" to give a clear image of your desired output beforehand.
    * **Effect:** Highly effective when you want to ensure adherence to a specific tone & manner (style or atmosphere) or format.

### 2. "Basic Techniques" for Accurately Conveying Intent

Techniques to set up prerequisites so AI can work without confusion.

* **Give a clear role**
    * **Method:** Declare at the beginning: "You are an [experienced HR consultant / skilled data analyst / professional copywriter]."
    * **Effect:** AI understands which expertise and perspective to use, bringing output quality closer to professional level.
* **Share background and purpose (context)**
    * **Method:** Write "For whom" and "For what purpose" the information is needed. (Example: "I want to explain the purpose of implementing a new system in an easy-to-understand way for junior employees.")
    * **Effect:** You get customized responses tailored to your situation, rather than general ones.
* **Specify output format concretely**
    * **Method:** Clearly define the shape of the output: "in table format," "in 3 bullet points," "in email text," "in markdown format," etc.
    * **Effect:** Makes it easier to copy-paste the response directly into work (documents, emails), reducing editing work.
* **Set constraints (things to avoid)**
    * **Method:** Specify what you don't want: "Don't use jargon," "Within 500 characters," "Don't create deeply nested bullet points," etc.

---

### 3. "Practical Dialogue Techniques" for Streamlining Daily Work

Tips for collaborating with AI rather than ending with a single Q&A.

* **Feedback loop (brainstorming)**
    * **Method:** Don't expect a perfect answer in one go. Give iterative correction instructions like "Make the tone softer," "Add more perspective on XX and rewrite."
    * **Effect:** By guiding AI like a supervisor edits a subordinate's work, the final quality is assured.
* **Structuring and format conversion of long text**
    * **Method:** Paste raw meeting notes and instruct: "Please organize this content into a table with 'Decisions,' 'ToDos,' and 'Items to carry over to next meeting.'"
    * **Effect:** Fully utilizes AI's strength in "information organization and summarization," significantly reducing document creation time.
* **Deliberately request multiple options**
    * **Method:** Instruct: "Please propose **5 completely different approaches** for the catchphrase."
    * **Effect:** Quickly gather creative ideas beyond your own thinking framework.

By combining these techniques, you can create powerful prompts such as:

~~~
# Role
You are an excellent marketer.

# Purpose
To create a product plan document,

# Counter-question
Please ask me questions about the necessary items.

# Output
Finally, summarize in table format.
~~~
