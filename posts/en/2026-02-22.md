# Perplexity Integrated into Samsung Galaxy AI

Samsung has announced the integration of Perplexity (an AI search engine) into Galaxy AI on the latest Galaxy S26. Users can now invoke Perplexity alongside traditional Bixby and Gemini by using the voice command "Hey, Plex."

This move is part of Samsung's initiative to build a **"multi-agent ecosystem"** (a system where multiple AIs work together). Rather than relying on a single AI assistant, the company aims to enhance user experience by creating an environment where users can select the optimal AI tool based on their needs.

*Impact on our lives*: Smartphones will offer more flexible and diverse AI features to choose from, making search and information retrieval more efficient.

## OpenAI Strengthens Monitoring of ChatGPT Misuse

Following reports that a Canadian shooting suspect engaged in conversations about gun violence on ChatGPT, it was revealed that OpenAI had considered notifying police. The suspect's statements had been flagged by ChatGPT's misuse monitoring tool.

This case highlights the critical issue of **balancing AI companies' social responsibility with privacy protection**. While AI platforms must detect harmful content, they face ethical questions about when and how to report to law enforcement.

*Impact on our lives*: While prevention of AI misuse will be strengthened, discussions about privacy will become even more active.

## AI Industry Survival: Google Issues Warning

A Google VP warned that **"LLM wrappers" and "AI aggregators"—two types of AI startups—are facing an existential crisis** as generative AI (LLMs) continue to evolve.

LLM wrappers are services that merely add functionality to existing models, while AI aggregators are services that integrate multiple AI tools. These startups face declining profit margins and difficulty in differentiation, potentially losing long-term competitiveness.

*Impact on our lives*: Market consolidation in the AI industry will accelerate, leaving only truly innovative and differentiated AI services to survive.

## Microsoft's New Gaming CEO Pushes Back Against "AI Saturation"

Microsoft's new gaming division CEO has declared that the gaming ecosystem will not be filled with **"AI saturation"** (mass deployment of low-quality AI-generated content).

While AI adoption is gaining attention in the gaming industry, low-quality AI implementation risks damaging player experience. Microsoft has signaled its commitment to balanced AI integration, a stance that could influence industry-wide AI policies.

*Impact on our lives*: Game quality will be preserved and responsible AI technology adoption will advance, delivering a better entertainment experience.

## India's Sarvam Launches Indus AI Chat App

Sarvam, an Indian AI startup, has launched a new chat app called "Indus AI" in beta. While global players like ChatGPT and Google Gemini dominate the Asia-Pacific region, development of AI services tailored to regional characteristics is rapidly accelerating.

This **regional AI development movement** demonstrates demand for AI services optimized for local languages and cultures, promoting diversification of the global AI market.

*Impact on our lives*: AI services optimized for our languages and cultures will become more accessible, expanding choices for more usable AI tools.

## Summary

As of February 2026, the AI industry is undergoing major transformation. On one hand, major companies like Samsung and Microsoft are strategically advancing AI integration and accelerating the transition to multi-agent ecosystems. On the other hand, corporate emphasis on social responsibility and AI safety—exemplified by OpenAI—is strengthening, increasing the importance of AI governance.

Simultaneously, **consolidation of poorly differentiated AI startups and intensifying regional AI development competition in countries like India** are underway. These developments represent not merely technological evolution in AI, but a fundamental reconsideration of how AI should be integrated into society.

Going forward, what matters most for users will be developing the ability to discern which AI services truly enrich our lives, while simultaneously raising awareness about ethics and privacy in AI usage.
