## Work Remaining for Companies in the AI Era: "Decision-Making"

With rapid AI advancement, automation of conventional routine tasks is accelerating. However, industry experts are noting that the work remaining for companies in the AI era is "judgment."

Simple data processing and routine operations are left to AI, while strategic decision-making and areas requiring complex judgment are positioned as work only humans can perform. This shift will require employees to develop different skill sets.

**Impact on Our Lives:** The shift toward an era where critical thinking and judgment hold value rather than simple labor is accelerating, dramatically changing the direction of human resource development.
