## Open-Source AI "OpenClaw" Fan Event a Huge Success

"ClawCon," held in New York, is a unique event bringing together fans of the open-source AI project "OpenClaw." The venue was decorated with **lobster-themed décor**, and participants deepened their connections under purple and pink lighting.

The open-source AI community (AI technology that anyone can freely use and improve) is growing as a counterculture to corporate-led, closed AI development. This event symbolizes an attitude that values **community bonds** alongside technology, representing an important movement promoting the democratization of AI development.

## AI Tennis Machine "Tenniix" Hosting Experience Event in Tokyo

The much-talked-about AI tennis machine "Tenniix," which surpassed **100 million yen on crowdfunding site Makuake**, will hold an experience event on March 9 near Hatsudai Station in Tokyo.

This machine is equipped with a function that uses AI to analyze players' levels and movements, automatically adjusting ball speed and trajectory to optimal levels. Unlike conventional tennis machines, its standout feature is **the sense of realism comparable to playing against a human opponent**. It's garnering attention not only from tennis enthusiasts but also as a practice partner for beginners.

*Impact on our lives*: AI-equipped sports equipment enables individuals to engage in efficient training, lowering barriers to sports participation.

## ChatGPT-Equipped Smartwatch on Limited-Time Sale

The smartwatch "QS40 Generation 3," equipped with AI watch face generation and ChatGPT functionality, is currently on sale at an **early-bird price of 7,382 yen**, down from its regular price of 10,980 yen.

The product's greatest feature is the ability to **interact directly with ChatGPT on your wrist**. AI also analyzes health data, providing improvement recommendations based on sleep quality and activity levels. Furthermore, the AI-generated watch face designs change based on user preferences and mood, allowing for a fresh experience every day.

The integration of AI into wearable devices represents an important step toward realizing a **"next-generation personal assistant"** that enables information access and AI interaction without taking out your smartphone.

*Impact on our lives*: Easy access to AI assistants makes answering everyday questions and managing health more convenient and accessible.
