## OpenAI CEO Altman Discusses the Nearness of AGI Realization

OpenAI CEO Sam Altman made important remarks about the future of the AI industry in an interview in India. He stated that **the realization of AGI (Artificial General Intelligence) and ASI (Artificial Super Intelligence) is approaching**, positioning AI infrastructure development as "the largest project in human history." He also took a critical view of the space data center concept, calling it "ridiculous." This provides an opportunity to consider realistic challenges in AI development and differing strategies within the industry.

[→ Read the original article (ITmedia AI+)](https://www.itmedia.co.jp/news/articles/2602/23/news014.html)

*Impact on our lives*: As AGI realization draws closer to reality, virtually all professions and lifestyles could undergo significant changes.

## Samsung Integrates Perplexity into Galaxy AI

Samsung Electronics is expanding AI assistant functionality in the Galaxy S26. **Samsung is integrating Perplexity, a search-based AI, into Galaxy AI**, allowing users to access it by saying "Hey, Plex." In addition to existing Bixby and Gemini, the company is advancing the construction of a *"multi-agent ecosystem"* where users can choose between multiple AI agents. This strategy of integrating technology from different AI companies aims to provide users with optimal AI experiences.

[→ Read the original article (The Verge)](https://www.theverge.com/tech/882921/samsung-is-adding-perplexity-to-galaxy-ai)

*Impact on our lives*: Smartphone voice assistants will become more multifunctional and accurate, making everyday information searches more efficient.

## ComfyUI Jointly Develops Anime-Specialized AI "Anima"

ComfyUI is advancing development of the image generation AI "Anima." This model **specializes in generating anime and illustration-style images**, aiming to exceed the quality of SDXL (Stable Diffusion XL), the current standard model. It will provide high-quality image generation capabilities sought by anime fans worldwide, particularly in Japan where anime culture is strong. As AI image generation tools become increasingly specialized and sophisticated, active development of superior models in niche fields is accelerating.

[→ Read the original article (ASCII.jp)](https://ascii.jp/elem/000/004/375/4375749/?rss)

*Impact on our lives*: Creators and anime fans will be able to easily generate high-quality images tailored to their preferences.

## Progress in Research Using AI to Discover New Antibiotics

Recent research has drawn attention to scientists' efforts to develop new antibiotics using AI. **"Molecular-level de-extinction," which restores antimicrobial molecules from the genetic codes of mammoths and Neanderthals**, represents how AI's power extends exploration beyond conventional boundaries. Facing the global challenge of antimicrobial resistance (AMR), an innovative approach has emerged that leverages ancient DNA data. This is not merely medical progress; it demonstrates a case where AI can solve problems from perspectives previously unimaginable.

[→ Read the original article (ASCII.jp)](https://ascii.jp/elem/000/004/375/4375750/?rss)

*Impact on our lives*: If antibiotic development accelerates, treatment options for infectious diseases will increase, potentially saving many patients' lives.

## Yann LeCun Warns of Platform Algorithm Bias

Yann LeCun, a pioneer in deep learning, introduced important research published in Nature on X (Twitter). The research shows that **certain social media platforms have the effect of pushing political positions further to the right**. In other words, platform algorithms may be intentionally or structurally guiding users toward particular political directions. This once again highlights how crucial the issues of AI and algorithm transparency and ethics are.

[→ Read the original article (Yann LeCun X post)](https://nitter.net/danielgoyal/status/2025649075840745881#m)

*Impact on our lives*: When using social media, it becomes important to be aware of whether algorithms are influencing your views.

As of February 2026, the AI industry is entering a **phase of rapid integration and specialization**. OpenAI's Altman speaks of AGI realization's nearness, Samsung is building multi-agent AI ecosystems, while warnings about algorithms' societal impact are strengthening.
