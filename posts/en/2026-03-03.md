## Cursor Surpasses $2 Billion in Annualized Revenue: AI Development Tool Experiences Explosive Growth

AI code assistance tool "Cursor" has reportedly surpassed **$2 billion in annualized revenue** (approximately 300 billion yen). Revenue has doubled over the past three months, representing an extraordinary growth rate.

Cursor is a tool that leverages large language models (LLMs) to automate and accelerate developers' coding tasks. It is rapidly gaining support from developers for the following characteristics:

- **AI-native design**: Rather than retrofitting traditional editors with AI capabilities, it is built from the ground up with AI at its core
- **Context awareness**: Provides code suggestions while understanding the entire project
- **Productivity enhancement**: Significantly reduces developers' work time

The rapid adoption of such AI development tools is accelerating efficiency improvements across the entire software industry, fundamentally changing the nature of programming work.
