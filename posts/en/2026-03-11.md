## Meta Acquires "Moltbook," an SNS for AI Agents

Meta has acquired "Moltbook," a social network dedicated to AI. The platform specializes in interactions between AI agents (artificially intelligent systems that operate autonomously), and the development team will join Meta's next-generation AI division.

Meta plans to continue the service for now, but has hinted that Moltbook may cease operations as a standalone service in the future due to technology integration. This move demonstrates Meta's commitment to building an ecosystem that promotes AI activities across enterprises.

**Impact on Our Lives:** As AI agent technology develops, automated task support will become more sophisticated, accelerating workplace efficiency improvements.

## Google Deeply Integrates Gemini into Workspace, Enabling AI Usage Across Applications

Google announced new features that deeply integrate its generative AI "Gemini" into Workspace applications (Documents, Sheets, etc.). This allows users to create content and search for information while cross-referencing emails and files.

The features will be rolled out starting from English-speaking regions to corporate beta testers and specific paid plan users, with general availability expected within several months. This integration significantly expands the scope of AI utilization in office work.

**Impact on Our Lives:** Business professionals' document creation and information organization tasks will become dramatically more efficient, freeing up time for more creative work.

## NVIDIA Makes Major Investment in Thinking Machines Lab Led by Murati

NVIDIA has entered into a multi-year strategic partnership with Thinking Machines Lab, led by Mira Murati, former CTO of OpenAI. The company will deploy next-generation AI systems at the 1GW (gigawatt) scale called "Vera Rubin," advancing the training of cutting-edge models and the development of customizable AI platforms.

NVIDIA is also making a major investment in the company, clearly demonstrating its commitment to strengthening AI computational infrastructure and accelerating model development. Large-scale computational resources like Vera Rubin enable the development of more advanced and complex AI models.

**Impact on Our Lives:** The development of more advanced and practical AI models will accelerate, enabling successive implementations of AI applications across various fields such as healthcare and scientific research.

## Microsoft Files Amicus Brief Supporting Anthropic in DoD Litigation

Microsoft has filed an amicus brief as a corporation supporting AI startup Anthropic in its lawsuit against the U.S. Department of Defense (DoD).

Anthropic has been prohibited from using its products by the DoD due to product terms of service that restrict military use of AI. Microsoft has warned that this measure negatively impacts the AI industry and U.S. military operations, demonstrating its support for Anthropic from a legal perspective. AI companies are increasingly engaging in important discussions surrounding AI usage regulations and ethical considerations.

**Impact on Our Lives:** As legal discussions regarding AI utilization restrictions advance, society as a whole may develop clearer ethical guidelines for AI use.

## Fortinet Unveils AI Operational Security "FortiAIGate"

Security company Fortinet has announced "FortiAIGate," an advanced AI runtime security gateway that protects LLMs (large language models) from prompt injection, data leakage, model theft, and overconsumption.

This gateway serves as critical security infrastructure for enterprises to deploy AI safely, efficiently, and at scale. As AI rapidly proliferates, security threats are also increasing, raising the necessity for such defensive technologies.

**Impact on Our Lives:** Companies will be able to adopt AI tools with greater confidence, further accelerating AI's integration into society.

## Clinical Knowledge AI "Cubec" for Physicians Receives Update

Cubec Inc., a venture founded by the National Cerebral and Cardiovascular Center, has updated its clinical knowledge AI "Cubec" for physicians, incorporating three operational modes: "speed," "reasoning," and "papers." Depending on clinical needs, physicians can select a mode that prioritizes rapid responses, provides detailed medical reasoning, or is based on the latest academic papers.

Additionally, the company has expanded its proprietary database "Cubec Medical Notes" by approximately 300 entries, further contributing to physicians' knowledge exploration and work efficiency. AI utilization in the medical field is making concrete progress.

**Impact on Our Lives:** Physician diagnostic support will become more accurate, potentially improving healthcare quality and providing better services to patients.

## "Squadbase," an AI-Generated Data Analysis Platform Evolved for Non-Engineers

"Squadbase," provided by Queue Inc., underwent a functional update in November 2025 as an AI-native data analysis platform. Designed with generative AI as a foundation, this service allows non-engineer business users to perform data analysis and build internal applications using only natural language.

This democratizes business intelligence (BI) and analytics work, enabling employees without specialized knowledge to conduct advanced data analysis independently.
