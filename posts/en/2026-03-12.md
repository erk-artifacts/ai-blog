# Google Completes Record-Breaking Wiz Acquisition—Accelerating AI Security Integration

Google has completed its acquisition of cloud and AI security company **Wiz** for $32 billion. This is Google's largest acquisition to date as a standalone company.

Following the acquisition, Wiz will be **integrated into Google Cloud**, while maintaining brand independence and continuing to provide services to multi-cloud environments including AWS (Amazon Web Services) and Azure (Microsoft).

This integration aims to achieve:

- Fusion of Wiz's security expertise with Google's **AI (Artificial Intelligence) technology**
- Dramatic reduction in response time to cyberattacks
- Cost savings in enterprise operations

As cloud diversification accelerates, demand for AI-driven security solutions is rapidly growing, making this acquisition a strategic response to such market needs.

**Impact on Our Lives**: Enterprise and personal cloud data will be protected by more advanced AI technology, improving the security of digital assets.

## Meta Unveils Latest Four Models of In-House AI Chip "MTIA"—Accelerating Mega Data Center Plans

Meta has released four new models in its **custom AI chip series "MTIA"** optimized for AI-related tasks. Targeting optimization of inference processing (prediction and generation using trained models) and cost reduction, the company has dramatically shortened its development cycle compared to previous iterations.

In parallel, Meta is pursuing:

- Construction of massive clusters with large quantities of NVIDIA GPUs
- Plans to operate ultra-large data centers called "**Hyperion**" with future power consumption at **5GW scale** (gigawatt scale)

These facilities will provide the enormous computational resources needed for training and inference of generative AI (AI that automatically generates text, images, audio, etc.) models. Through development of proprietary chips, Meta is pursuing a strategy to reduce hardware dependency and strengthen competitiveness.

**Impact on Our Lives**: You'll be able to enjoy faster and more efficient AI services (including metaverse platforms).

## Anthropic Establishes Specialized Think Tank for AI Risk Research—Supporting Policy Decisions

AI safety research company **Anthropic** has established a new division called "**Anthropic Institute**" to research AI's societal impact. It will analyze risks advanced AI poses to the economy and employment, supporting government policy decisions.

To strengthen its engagement with policymakers, Anthropic is **establishing an office in Washington, D.C.** As large language models (LLM: massive language processing AI trained on vast text data) develop, the company will provide scientifically-grounded recommendations and proposals to address challenges such as job losses and economic inequality.

As U.S. government regulatory discussions deepen regarding supply chain risk designations, Anthropic is strengthening information disclosure and collaboration with external organizations, aiming to contribute to building trust across the AI industry.

**Impact on Our Lives**: Greater awareness of societal risks (such as unemployment) accompanying AI development, and the establishment of a foundation for implementing countermeasures.

## Zendesk Acquires Agentic AI Startup "Forethought"

Customer service platform company **Zendesk** has acquired **Forethought**, a startup specializing in agentic AI (AI that acts and decides autonomously). Forethought specializes in automated customer service tasks and won TechCrunch's "Battlefield" competition in 2018, making it a notable startup.

Through this acquisition, Zendesk's customer service platform will be integrated with **agentic AI**, enabling more sophisticated automated responses. Evolved from chatbots (conversational AI), "agents" can automatically execute multiple workflow steps, reducing human intervention while improving customer satisfaction.

**Impact on Our Lives**: Faster customer service response times and widespread 24-hour automated support will make customer inquiries to companies more efficiently processed.

## Ford Launches AI-Powered Fleet Management Assistant "Ford Pro AI"

Automaker **Ford** has announced "**Ford Pro AI**," an AI assistant for managing vehicle fleets (large collections of business vehicles). This feature provides fleet owners (corporate transportation and logistics departments, etc.) with real-time monitoring of safe driving data such as seatbelt usage.

"Ford Pro AI" was first unveiled at **Work Truck Week** (held in Indianapolis) and is now available to all subscribers of the **Ford Pro telematics (remote data transmission) service** based in the United States.

This AI assistant achieves:

- Monitoring driver safety behavior
- Predicting risk of accidents and violations
- Streamlining fleet-wide management

Contributing to improved safety and reduced operating costs in the transportation industry.

**Impact on Our Lives**: As delivery and transportation services become safer, you'll enjoy more reliable delivery services.

## NTT Docomo Announces "6G for AI and Robots" Vision—Calling for Cross-Industry Collaboration

NTT Docomo of Japan has announced its "**6G for AI and Robots**" vision at MWC26 (Mobile World Congress). Addressing challenges difficult for the telecommunications industry alone to manage—such as future explosive growth in AI communication volume and population decline—the company is advocating for the necessity of **cross-industry infrastructure collaboration**.

At the exhibition, Docomo showcased:

- A pen-type terminal conceived by Yoichi Ochiai (media artist)
- Sensorless robots

Materializing the company's vision of a future society where AI and robots thrive. 6G (sixth-generation mobile communication system) is expected to be realized in the 2030s, delivering ultra-high-speed, ultra-low-latency communication far exceeding conventional 5G.

**Impact on Our Lives**: The convergence of AI technology, robots, and next-generation communications will make AI application scenarios such as autonomous driving, remote medicine, and smart cities more feasible.

## Electrical Brain Stimulation Increases "Altruistic Behavior"—Swiss Research Team Confirms

Researchers from the University of Zurich and other institutions have published research findings demonstrating that **applying weak electrical stimulation to the brain through the scalp can promote altruistic behavior (actions benefiting others)**.

This research represents convergence of neuroscience with AI and machine learning (pattern recognition and prediction), leading to new understanding of human behavior. Through clarifying neurological mechanisms, the following applications are anticipated in the future:

- Neuroscientific approaches to building altruistic societies
- Improved AI accuracy in predicting human behavior
- Personalized treatment based on brain activity

**Impact on Our Lives**: The convergence of neuroscience and AI will advance technology to more deeply understand and predict human behavior and emotion, with applications in psychotherapy and community building.
