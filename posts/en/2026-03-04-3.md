## Political Resistance Movement Against AI Intensifies

Approximately 90 U.S. political and community leaders held a secret conference in New Orleans in early January. Reports indicate that a "Pro-Human AI Declaration" movement has begun, aimed at discussing the ethical deployment and regulation of AI technology. This initiative promotes dialogue between AI development companies and civil society, bringing together multiple perspectives on the impact AI has on society as a whole.

The conference brought together individuals from diverse backgrounds, including labor union leaders, political activists, and consumer rights advocates. This represents an important effort amid the intersection of concerns and expectations regarding the rapid development of AI technology.

**Impact on Our Lives:** A system is beginning to take shape that allows a broader range of citizens to have their voices reflected in decisions about AI development and operation.

## AI Analyzes Earth's Natural Sounds, Deepening Understanding of Climate Change

A project is underway to analyze various sounds emitted by the Earth—earthquakes, glacier collapses, wildfires, and storms—using AI technology. These acoustic data provide important information for predicting climate change and natural disasters. Machine learning algorithms now make it possible to discover patterns in natural phenomena that conventional observation methods could not capture.

This initiative is attracting attention as a new approach to Earth environmental monitoring and demonstrates the rapid expansion of AI applications in climate science.

**Impact on Our Lives:** More precise weather forecasting and early warning systems for natural disasters are coming closer to reality.

## No-Code Development Platform "Raycast Glaze" Launches

By leveraging AI tools like Claude (LLMs: Large Language Models), even general users without coding knowledge can now develop applications. "Glaze," a new service offered by Raycast, is a platform where users can share "Vibe Code" (code generated by AI).

This democratization is ushering in an era where non-technical individuals and small organizations can develop and distribute their own software. However, questions about security and quality assurance are also emerging simultaneously.

**Impact on Our Lives:** The speed at which individual and small group creativity is transformed into digital tools is dramatically accelerating.

## Robot "NICOBO" Aiming for Coexistence with Humans Announces Next Development

A robotics company held a presentation announcing the new direction for "NICOBO" (Niko-bo), a robot designed to bring smiles. Research is progressing to make the interaction between AI and humans more natural and approachable. Guest speakers expressed expectations for everyday coexistence scenarios, such as "wanting to have drinks together at home," and a future is becoming visible where robotics will be integrated into living spaces beyond the entertainment sector.

**Impact on Our Lives:** Household robots may shift from being merely mechanical assistants to becoming "family members."

## Summary

In the AI industry in early March 2026, the following major trends are evident:

- **Ethics and Governance:** The momentum is accelerating for multiple stakeholders to discuss the social impact of AI
- **Expansion of Application Areas:** AI applications are advancing in Earth science, environmental monitoring, and various aspects of daily life
- **Democratization:** An environment is being established where AI technology can be utilized without coding knowledge
- **Humanization of Robotics:** Development of AI robots that resonate with human emotions is progressing

These movements suggest that AI is transitioning from a mere "technology" to a "social infrastructure." At the same time, attention must be paid to new challenges (ethics, safety, employment impact, and more).
