## Japan's Defense Ministry Begins Using Generative AI for Diet Deliberation Materials

The Ministry of Defense has begun a pilot program utilizing generative AI (generative artificial intelligence) to create draft materials for Diet deliberation responses. In-house volunteers developed the tool, and operations began in some departments from the 221st Special Diet session convened on February 18. The ministry aims to improve efficiency and accuracy while considering expanding usage across the entire organization.

## CoeFont Deploys AI Translation Service for Inbound Tourists
