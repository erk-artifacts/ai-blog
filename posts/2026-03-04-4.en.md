## Secret Meeting of AI Political Movement Held——Industry Regulatory Policy to Shift

In early January, approximately 90 politicians and community leaders held a confidential AI conference at a Marriott hotel in New Orleans. The conference aimed to form a more proactive political opposition stance in response to growing concerns about the societal impact of AI (artificial intelligence).

Industry leaders and policymakers gathered to discuss regulatory policies for AI companies and AI governance. This movement reflects heightened societal concerns about the rapid development of AI.

Political-level responses are recognized as urgent regarding numerous challenges posed by AI development, including job losses, bias, and privacy violations.

## Offshore Floating Data Centers Lead New Development in AI Infrastructure

Offshore wind power development company "Aikido" announced plans to deploy AI data centers beneath floating offshore wind turbines. Implementation is scheduled for 2026.

This approach realizes AI computational infrastructure construction at **offshore** locations rather than **space-based data centers**. By directly connecting with renewable energy sources, it supports AI learning and inference processing that requires massive power consumption while reducing environmental impact.

With the advancement of AI technology, data center power demand is surging, and this represents an innovative solution.

**Impact on Our Lives:** As AI service infrastructure evolves toward environmental consideration, the realization of a more sustainable AI society draws closer.

## Robot "NICOBO" Announces Next Step in Coexistence with Humans

Smile Robot company announced new developments for AI-equipped robot "NICOBO" toward human and societal symbiosis. The announcement event, which featured comedian Hikoro Hee as a guest, demonstrated the possibilities of daily communication and emotional interaction with robots.

NICOBO is equipped with **natural conversation capabilities** and **emotion recognition AI**, and is expected to be utilized as a partner robot in homes. At the announcement event, it was evaluated as an endearing robot, with comments such as "I'd like to have a drink at home with it."

This suggests a new lifestyle brought about by the integration of robotics and AI, particularly **natural language processing** and **emotion recognition**.

**Impact on Our Lives:** As AI robots become mainstream as home partner companions, care support and emotional assistance in an aging society may be realized.
