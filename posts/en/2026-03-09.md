## Summary

As of March 2026, AI technology implementation and demonstration are progressing across diverse fields in Japan, from government administration to corporate operations, game development, and communication infrastructure. Three key points warrant particular attention:

1. **Japan's Domestic AI Development**: Implementation and demonstration of the Digital Agency's "Gennai" with 180,000 users will rapidly accelerate the foundation-building of Japan's domestic AI industry.

2. **Sector-Specific Practical Applications**: AI integration is becoming concrete in specialized fields such as lawyer-focused document creation and game development.

3. **Balance Between Security and Convenience**: While AI proliferation introduces new threats such as ransomware attacks, improvements in smartphone functionality and infrastructure efficiency are also being realized.

Going forward, the shift from being "AI users" to "AI developers" and cross-industry standardization will become critical factors determining Japan's competitive advantage in AI.
