# Systematic Prompting Techniques: Elevating AI from a Search Tool to a Professional Assistant

This time, I've systematically compiled practical tips and techniques to help ordinary office workers elevate AI from "just a search tool" to a "skilled assistant."

---

## 1. Advanced Techniques to Unlock AI's Potential

First, here are techniques like the one you suggested—where small adjustments can dramatically improve accuracy.

* **Having AI Create Prompts (Meta-prompting) ※Introduced in a previous article.**
    * **Method:** Give the instruction: "I want to create a business proposal about 〇〇. Please create a prompt (instruction) format that I should input so you can produce the best possible output."
    * **Effect:** AI identifies necessary elements you might not have thought of (target audience, budget, timeline, etc.), resulting in higher-quality output.

* **Having AI Ask Counter-Questions**
    * **Method:** Add this to the end of your instruction: "**If there is any missing information or unclear points when executing this instruction, please ask me questions.**"
    * **Effect:** AI points out gaps in your instructions, preventing off-target responses.

* **Making AI Think Step-by-Step (Chain of Thought)**
    * **Method:** When requesting complex problem-solving or calculations, add: "**Please think through this logically, step by step.**"
    * **Effect:** AI breaks down its thought process, dramatically improving the accuracy and persuasiveness of conclusions.

* **Providing Examples (Few-shot Prompting)**
    * **Method:** Give instructions like: "Please produce output following these examples: [Example 1] [Example 2]" to set expectations for the desired output.
    * **Effect:** Highly effective when you want to enforce specific tone & manner (writing style and atmosphere) or particular formats.

---

## 2. Foundational Techniques for Communicating Your Intent Clearly

Techniques to set preconditions so AI can work without confusion.

* **Assigning a Clear Role**
    * **Method:** State at the beginning: "You are an [experienced HR consultant / excellent data analyst / professional copywriter]."
    * **Effect:** AI knows which expertise and perspective to use, bringing output quality closer to professional standards.

* **Sharing Background and Purpose (Context)**
    * **Method:** Write "who this is for" and "why this information is needed." (Example: "I want to clearly explain the purpose of a new system implementation to junior staff members in my company.")
    * **Effect:** Receive customized answers tailored to your situation, not just generic information.

* **Specifying Output Format Concretely**
    * **Method:** Be explicit about output form: "in table format," "narrow it down to 3 bullet points," "as an email," "in markdown format," etc.
    * **Effect:** You can copy-paste the response directly into your work (documents, emails), reducing revision work.

* **Establishing Constraints (Things NOT to Do)**
    * **Method:** Specify what you don't want: "Don't use technical jargon," "Keep it under 500 characters," "Don't make bullet-point hierarchies too deep," etc.

---

## 3. Practical Dialogue Techniques for Streamlining Daily Work

Tips for ongoing collaboration with AI, not just one-off Q&A.

* **Feedback Loop (Rubber-ducking)**
    * **Method:** Don't expect a perfect answer the first time. Instead, give revision instructions for the output you receive: "Make the tone softer," "Add the 〇〇 perspective," "Rewrite this," etc.
    * **Effect:** By guiding AI like a manager reviewing a subordinate's work, you ensure final quality.

* **Structuring and Reformatting Long Text**
    * **Method:** Paste meeting notes as plain text and instruct: "Divide this content into 'Decisions,' 'Action Items,' and 'Carryover Issues for Next Meeting' and put it in a table."
    * **Effect:** Fully utilize AI's strength in "information organization and summarization," significantly reducing document creation time.

* **Deliberately Requesting Multiple Proposals**
    * **Method:** Give instructions like: "Propose **5 completely different approaches** for a catchphrase."
    * **Effect:** Quickly gather idea seeds that transcend your usual thinking patterns.

---

By combining these techniques, you can create powerful prompts like:

~~~
# Role
You are an excellent marketer.

# Purpose
To create a business proposal for a new product,

# Counter-questions
Please ask me for necessary items.

# Output
Finally, format it as a table.
~~~
